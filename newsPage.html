There’s more bad news for Terry’s Chocolate Orange fans

If you’re a fan of cracking open a Terry’s Chocolate Orange to snack on at Christmas time (and who isn’t?), we’re afraid we have some bad news for you.
The confectionery favourite is 36% more expensive now than it was last year, according to research by The Grocer. While the item has often been priced at £1 in the past, a 75g ball now retails at £1.34 on average.
While some supermarkets have raised the price of the item in their stores, you can still pick one up for £1 at Asda, or even better – 90p at Morrisons.
